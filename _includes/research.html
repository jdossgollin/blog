Research Interests

The overarching goal of my research is to develop and demonstrate an integrated framework for building a portfolio of structural and financial instruments for managing water and climate risk. To do this I am working on specific methods for building understanding, predictions, and plans where gaps in existing approaches are identified, and applying these methods to case studies relevant to sustainable development of urban, water, and energy systems.
